Wine Not?

An analysis of wine data that compares how several variables affect price.

Price vs Vintage

There is an obvious correllation between price and vintage. It appears that as a wine ages, its value increases. However, through our extended research, we discovered that most wines are intended to be consumed within 10 years of purchase. So, buying a fine wine and letting it "age" might increase its value, but it does not increase its palatability.
